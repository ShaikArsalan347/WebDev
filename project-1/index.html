FAQ

What materials are your furniture pieces made from?

Answer:

Our furniture is crafted from a variety of high-quality materials, including solid wood, engineered wood, metal, glass, and premium upholstery fabrics. Each material is selected for its durability, aesthetic appeal, and ability to enhance the overall design of the piece.

How do I care for and maintain my furniture?

Answer:

Proper care depends on the material of your furniture. For wood pieces, we recommend using a soft, damp cloth for cleaning and avoiding harsh chemicals. Upholstered items should be vacuumed regularly and spot-cleaned as needed. We also offer care guides specific to each material, which can be found on the product page.

Do you offer customization options?

Answer:

Yes, many of our furniture pieces can be customized to suit your preferences. This may include selecting different fabrics, finishes, or dimensions. Customization options are detailed on each product page, or you can contact our customer service team for more information.

What is your return policy?

Answer:

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your furniture, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange. Please note that the item must be in its original condition, and some customized items may be non-returnable.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Answer:

Delivery times vary based on the product and your location. In-stock items typically ship within 7-10 business days. For custom or made-to-order pieces, delivery may take 4-6 weeks. You can find estimated delivery times on each product page.
