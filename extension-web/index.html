FAQ

What is Tab Time Tracker?

Answer:

Tab Time Tracker is a Browser extension designed to help users monitor the time spent on various websites throughout the day. It provides insights into browsing habits and productivity.

How does Tab Time Tracker work?

Answer:

The extension tracks the amount of time spent on each open tab in your browser. It collects this data and presents it in an easy-to-read format, allowing you to see which websites you visit the most.

Do I need to keep the extension open for it to track time?

Answer:

No, once installed, Tab Time Tracker runs in the background and automatically tracks your time spent on websites without needing to keep the extension's interface open.

Can I see time spent on previous days?

Answer:

Yes, you can navigate to previous days using the "Previous" button, which allows you to view the time spent on websites for any selected date.

Is my browsing data private?

Answer:

Yes, your browsing data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with any external servers. The extension respects your privacy and only tracks your time spent on websites.
